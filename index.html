Fabrics

For an everyday, comfortable aesthetic, natural fibers like breathable cotton and lightweight linen are ideal, offering a relaxed vibe perfect for casual outings or warm weather. A luxurious and elegant aesthetic is best achieved with the smooth texture and natural sheen of silk or satin, which drape beautifully and are the go-to fabrics for formal events and evening wear. For a light, romantic, and flowing aesthetic, sheer fabrics like chiffon and georgette are preferred, adding movement and an ethereal touch to dresses. In cooler months, a rich and cozy aesthetic can be created using plush velvet or warm knit fabrics, which provide both insulation and a sophisticated texture.
